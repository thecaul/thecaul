The Threshold

The Practitioner

JOSHUA
CAMPBELL

"I facilitate from inside my own ongoing search. Not from a place of completion."

For eighteen years I held space behind the chair. People would sit down, exhale, and something would open. Not because of what I did to their hair. Because of what happened in the room. They felt seen. That is where I learned to hold space for a human being in their most unguarded moments.

I am a practitioner of the shamanic arts based in Richmond, Virginia. My journey cracked open in Tulum. My formal training was in Portugal. I studied Reiki here in Richmond. All of it woven together into a practice built around one thing: facilitating the conditions for your own remembering.

The Caul is the veil that sometimes covers a child at birth, believed across many traditions to mark the soul as one who can move between worlds. This is the lens through which I work. Pain as teacher. Threshold as medicine. The body as a map of the unfinished.

I am a queer man. This work holds space for anyone who feels the pull toward something the western world never had a name for. You do not have to explain yourself here. You only have to be willing to cross.

The Sessions

THRESHOLD

Discovery
Call

30 minutes — $11

For the curious. The not-yet-sure.

Something is moving in you. You can feel it but maybe cannot name it yet. You have found yourself here and something about it feels right, but you are not sure where you fit or what you need. This call is for that person. We sit together, we talk, and we figure out what you are bumping up against and whether this work is the right door for you.

Some people leave with a clear next step. Some leave with a name for something they have been carrying. Some book a session. Some just needed to be heard.

Centering
Session

30 minutes

For those who feel pulled from their center.

Thirty minutes. Just you and me. You are dealing with something specific. A situation, a decision, a feeling you cannot shake. You do not need a diagnosis or a treatment plan. You need someone to sit with you, hear what is alive in you right now, and help you find the thread that leads you back to yourself. We talk. We reflect. I may offer practices to carry with you or a deeper path we begin together.

Think of it as therapy without the hour, without the clinical distance. Just presence, reflection, and a way back to your own center.

Guided
Journey 1:1

60 minutes

For those ready to go inside and meet themselves.

Using drum rhythm and visualization, I guide you into your own inner landscape. You do not need to believe in anything. Everything you are looking for is already inside you. This is how we go find it. People come to journeys seeking clarity, reconnection, answers about how to move forward. Some meet a spirit animal. Some find a part of themselves they had left behind.

What you find there is yours. The drum holds the door open. I lead. You explore your inner landscape.

Energetic
Alignment

60 minutes

For those who feel energetically off. Stagnant. Heavy. Stuck.

You cannot always name it. You just know something feels off. There is a dullness, a weight, a discomfort that lives somewhere in the body and will not move. Through Reiki and shamanic energy work, I check in with your energetic body to find what has gone still and help it move again. Blockages released. Chakras brought back into balance. The nervous system reminded of what calm actually feels like.

Some people leave feeling lighter than they have in months. Some notice shifts in the days that follow. The body knows what it needs.

Reflection and
Integration

60 minutes

For those sitting with what a journey brought up.

The session is not where the work ends. It is where it begins. Something surfaced in your journey. Images, feelings, information, a knowing you are still sitting with. This is the time to bring it into the light. We look at it together. We make sense of what it means for your life. I offer integration steps so what arose in the inner landscape can take root in the real one.

The journey gives you the material. Integration is how you build something with it.

The discovery call is $11 and is non-refundable. For Seeker tier clients this $11 applies toward your first session. For all other tiers it does not carry forward. If you are a Seeker tier client and miss your discovery call without notice, that $11 is forfeited.

Cancellation Policy

A cancellation is not just a calendar event. It is lost time, lost energy, and lost income. Please honor your commitment to this work and to yourself.

48 hours or more notice — no charge.

Less than 48 hours notice — 50% of the session cost.

No-call no-show — 100% of the session cost.

Payment is required to confirm your session. Please send payment via Venmo or Zelle within 24 hours of booking. Your session is not held until payment is received.

THE CIRCLE

The Circle

There is a threshold most men never cross. Not because they lack the courage. Because no one ever held the door open for them.

This is that door.

Built by a queer man. Held for queer men, queer adjacent men, and any man who feels like the boxes were never built for him.

We are not here to perform masculinity. We are here to remember it. The kind that lives beneath the armor. Divine, not dominant. Present, not proved.

I know what it is to move through a world that was not made for you. I know what it is to sit in a men's circle and still feel unseen. This space exists because that experience deserves an answer.

You do not have to be queer to belong here. You just have to feel like this was made for you.

If you feel that pull, it was.
